Volunteer With Us

We can’t do it without you

We are thrilled you want to volunteer with us. Our volunteers are the backbone of our rescue.
We can't wait to introduce you to our organization and the team that makes it all happen. Please take a few minutes to read through the website. Learn our foster and adoption processes (no quizzes...we promise), and read the stories of the animals whose lives have been changed through Doggie Haven. When you're ready, please fill out our Volunteer Application.

Doggie Haven relies on volunteers in all of our programs. Saving and changing lives for good is hard work, and we couldn’t do it without you!
