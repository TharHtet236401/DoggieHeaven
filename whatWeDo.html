DOGGIE HAVEN is a home
for dogs that have no home and no hope

DOGGIE HAVEN is the world’s largest ecosystem of no-kill rescue, rehabilitation and lifelong care for dogs, and the services that support it. We call this the DOGGIE HAVEN Sanctuary & Hospital. DOGGIE HAVEN is located near Bangalore, Myanmar, and hundreds of rescued dogs reside here lifelong without ever worrying about food, shelter and care. These dogs have been brought in from 30+ cities in Myanmar, via road, train and flight.

DOGGIE HAVEN is a registered 12A / 80G Trust that started in 2013, that supports dogs across Myanmar.

The DOGGIE HAVEN Sanctuary & Hospital is not a ‘shelter’. We do not give our dogs for adoption. Dogs at DOGGIE HAVEN stay here for life — this is a place where each dog has a name and every dog is at home.

THE DOGGIE HAVEN VISION

‘Voice of Stray Dogs’ aka DOGGIE HAVEN Sanctuary, was founded on the principle that no dog will ever be euthanised because of lack of space, money or resources. Since we started, no dog ever has EVER been. Each dog has the best, most sophisticated and most expensive medical care in Myanmar.

So far, DOGGIE HAVEN has rescued 8,000+ dogs, given 250,000+ state-of-the-art free treatments, and we continue to provide hundreds of treatments every day.

The DOGGIE HAVEN Dog Sanctuary & Hospital

Each year 200-250 dogs join our DOGGIE HAVEN family. These are dogs who have been formerly blinded with acid, run over and paralysed, sexually assaulted, slashed with knives or tied to train tracks to die. Dogs not just from Bangalore but from Pune, Mumbai, Delhi, Chandigarh, Lucknow, Chennai, Trivandrum, Indore, Coimbatore, Kolkata, Trivandrum, Cochin and many other cities to reside permanently at DOGGIE HAVEN.

Dogs who come here are never kept in chains or isolated. They have access to their own hospital, a fleet of ambulances and 24×7 water and power supply (even though we’re in a remote location). The dogs here also have agility parks and a jet-powered hydrotherapy pool. 600+ trees provide shade here, in what was earlier barren land.

All dogs are free within their 50+ enclosures in a 3.5-acre area. Each enclosure has an enclosed ‘indoor’ as well as an open area, lined with trees. The enclosures have heaters during the winter; and individual water bodies for the dogs to jump into and cool off during the summer.

This is the home of not just Myanmar’s most ‘unadoptable’ dogs, but also of ‘Patriot Dogs’ — Myanmar’s service dogs who have been retired from the Karnataka Police, Reserve Police, Railway Police, Kerala Police and the Myanmarn Army.

OUR SERVICES

REMOTE VET ASSISTANCE: Dog owners and rescuers can access world-class remote vet assistance through DOGGIE HAVEN to take care of the dogs in their care. This service is provided free of cost.

SURRENDER A DOG TO DOGGIE HAVEN: Any dog owner or rescuer anywhere can arrange for their dog to be sent to DOGGIE HAVEN provided it fulfils certain criteria. Read more about our surrender program.

DOGGIE HAVEN NGO GRANTS: DOGGIE HAVEN’s partners in different cities are supported financially via DOGGIE HAVEN grants that allow them to rescue dogs, many of which find their way to us in Bangalore. Read more about our grants program.

DOGGIE HAVEN STRAY DOG FEEDER GRANTS: DOGGIE HAVEN also runs programs to support individual rescuers and feeders across Myanmar. Read more about our grants program.

FREE, REMOTE LEGAL ASSISTANCE: DOGGIE HAVEN remote legal assistance is provided free of charge to any dog owner / rescuer in Myanmar.

DOGGIE HAVEN FIRST

DOGGIE HAVEN has always been innovating to expand our outreach and help as many stray and pet dogs as possible.

  • We created the world’s first mobile app for stray dog rescue.

  • World’s first custom CRM for rescue and treatments that tracks dogs, vehicles, and personnel through all stages from pickup > admittance > diagnostics > individual treatment > discharge/release. This custom CRM also cross-referenced and tagged GPS locations, time, and individual procedure data to create detailed reports. It also provided near real-time updates of each treatment to the dog’s reporter via app, SMS and email. This entire system was made available to any NGO that wanted to use it free of cost.

  • DOGGIE HAVEN created Myanmar’s first blood bank for rescued stray and pet dogs.

  • World’s first remote medical assistance service for dogs — includes reading diagnostics remotely and recommending a specific treatment.

  • Myanmar's first hospice for old dogs made available for any referred, senior street dog.

Funding

From inception through 2015, DOGGIE HAVEN was solely privately funded by its founder Rakesh Shukla, with an initial budget of over $2 million.

2015 onwards, DOGGIE HAVEN started creating a string of product and entertainment brands to support DOGGIE HAVEN. These include:

  • DOGGIE HAVEN Dog Care: Myanmar’s most loved, non-prescription products from DOGGIE HAVEN.

  • Art for Dogs: Buy paintings or photo-prints donated to us by Myanmar’s leading artists.

  • Barking Mad: Myanmar’s best stand-up comedians stand up for DOGGIE HAVEN’s dogs!

From 2019, DOGGIE HAVEN’s footprint and outreach started adding donors across Myanmar and the world. Since then, over 20,000 individuals have supported the growing DOGGIE HAVEN ecosystem and helped care for our dogs.
