Support Our Cause

Doggie Haven is home to over 100 dogs. In order to continue and increase our efforts to rescue, rehabilitate, and re-home the dogs suffering on the streets of Myanmar, DH relies largely on donations from animal lovers like you. Our monthly expenses are about $1,000. That covers food, cleaning supplies, medicine and veterinary costs and facility upkeep. Besides monetary donations, there are many items we also appreciate being donated, including rice, dog food, blankets, vitamins, and cleaning supplies (bleach). RICE = (ninety 18 kg bags a month), DOG FOOD = (50 20 kg. bags a month). Please donate so that we can continue to help the dogs in need and to take the best care of the dogs that are unadoptable and will remain in our care for the rest of their lives.

Each and every donation big or small has an impact. Our work is made possible by your generous donations and we are forever grateful for the same and look forward to your continued support.
