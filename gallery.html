Orientation of Volunteers

Setting volunteers up for success, team Doggie Haven conducted an orientation of volunteers who are passionate about providing care for animals. During the event, our volunteers underwent training on how improve the comfort and well-being of animals by providing progressive and compassionate care. They also participated in making of food bowls for the strays. We thank all our volunteers for involving passionately during the event and encouraging others to become involved as well.

Feed The Need

Team conducted its periodic public awareness campaign of feeding the strays at various locations in the Myanmar. We placed several bowls to serve the dogs with water and food. Through this campaign, we also created sensitivity towards street animals and encouraged commuters to adopt the responsibility of providing stray animals with basic necessities of living such as food and water for each and every stray.

Celebrate Your Birthday In A Charitable Way !

Doggie Haven for stray dogs was pleased to recently host the birthday celebration of Suzy with her friends. Just like them, if you are an animal lover, you can celebrate your birthday in the company of your furry friends too! This is a perfectly adorable idea to make your birthday memorable as you will find yourself surrounded by wonderful adventure, sheer fun, and incredible love of our friendly stray dogs. You’ll also feel like you’ve done something worth it on your special day!

Awareness Drives

Young children are impressionable, at DH we believe that engaging with the youngsters is an important step in raising adults who are not only sensitive to stray dogs but who will also proactively work towards their welfare. The prime focus of these awareness drives is to provide a space where the children can engage with the stray dogs in a positive and meaningful way. We have conducted more than 20 awareness camps so far in different schools in Yangon the children took a pledge to not harm street dogs and fed stray dogs in an around their school under the supervision of their teachers.

Vaccination Drives

Vaccination is important to prevent deaths from fatal and contagious canine diseases. Doggie Haven has conducted multiple vaccination drives at various locations in Myanmar. In total, we are able to vaccinate 100+ stray dogs. The vaccination drives are conducted with the assistance of our volunteers that have made them a grand success!
