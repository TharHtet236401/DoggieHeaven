Paw of Dog Adoption Center

Paws of Love is dedicated to finding forever homes for stray dogs, providing them with love, care, and a second chance at a happy life.

The center focuses on creating a nurturing environment where each dog receives individualized attention to address their unique needs.

Kyun Taw Road

San Chaung Township

Yangon

MON-SAT 10:30am - 5pm

HomeBound Hounds Recue Hub

HomeBound Hounds is a rescue center specifically designed to address the needs of stray dogs, focusing on their rehabilitation and successful integration into loving homes.

The center employs positive reinforcement training methods to help dogs overcome behavioral challenges and build trusting relationships with potential adopters.

Lay Daung Kan Road

Tamwe Township

Yangon

MON-SAT 10:30am - 5pm

Hopeful Hearts Canine Haven

Hopeful Hearts focuses on rescuing and rehabilitating stray dogs with a commitment to providing medical care, behavioral training, and emotional support.

The center believes in the transformative power of love and strives to heal both the physical and emotional wounds of each dog in their care.

Hantharwady Road

Kamayut Township

Yangon

MON-SAT 10:30am - 5pm

Wagging Tails Adoption Center

Wagging Tails is known for its warm and welcoming environment, designed to ease the transition for both dogs and adopters.

The center has spacious play areas where dogs can interact and showcase their personalities, helping potential adopters make informed decisions.

Kabar Aye Pagoda Road

Bahan Township

Yangon

MON-SAT 10:30am - 5pm
